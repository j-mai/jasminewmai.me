About Me

Hi, I'm Jasmine, and I am currently a sophomore at Dartmouth College. I plan on majoring in Computer Science modified with Digital Arts and minoring in Japanese and Human-Centered Design. I enjoy logically thinking through problems and being able come up with solutions that can have an impact on others, and for me, CS is the perfect medium to do so. I'm proficient in Python and Java, and I'm currently interested app development, virtual reality, and web development.

Currently, I am interested in how media and technology can be used to create empathy. I have seen how voices can be trampled upon and silenced. I've realized that our biases impair our willingness to listen to the stories and struggles of others. In our increasingly polarized society, it is hard to look past our prejudices to start and create these conversations. I believe that animation provides a special zone where we can start these stories, and I also think that technology like virtual and augmented reality have strong potential to help create a more connected and empathetic world.
